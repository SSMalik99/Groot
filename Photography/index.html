The "Groot"

This article is about Mr.Groot. Who is famous for his contribution into the wild-life photography from last 20 years. This website is just a reflection of his imagination. During the initial days of his life he was not able to manage himself in a world, where almost everything is depending upon the money and the game of luck. That's Why he want to create an environment, where he can share his life experiences and techniques, cause everyone is not able to pay for a institute or for a highly qualified professor. The content on this website is taken from the experience of Mr. Groot and other professor. Initally he want to join the armed forces , he tried hard but his efforts wasn't enough to join the armed forces. Then he decided to make his career in the field of education and worked almost four years as a teacher of mathematics in a private school.At that point of time he had enough money to live his life peacefully,but he didn't know why he is not getting the satisfaction.So after that he just join the world of photography.
